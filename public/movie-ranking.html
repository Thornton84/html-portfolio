Sarah's Top 3 Movies

my favourite movies of all time

Trains, Planes and Automobiles

A childhood favourite that I still quote many lines or scenes from now
Steve Martin and John Candy in a road trip comedy with a heart warming ending.

Wall-E

An animated movie of a dystopian future where humans have destroyed the earth through over consumption and taken to a giant cruise ship style spaceship that awaits the robotic clean up of earth.
The plot line is worryingly possible for our future and should be a stark wake up call to what we are doing to the planet and our bodies.

Fern Gulley: The Last Rainforest

Another amimated movie from my childhood with a deeper warning about human impact to the planet.
